Introduction to Information Technology (IT)

Welcome to your journey into the world of Information Technology. Checking email, streaming videos, online shopping, and remote work all rely on IT.

IT involves computers, networks, software, and digital systems used to store, process, send, and retrieve information. It powers nearly every sector, from healthcare and finance to education and entertainment.
